HP 8591E ROM — DLP (Downloadable Program Language) Programs
========================================================================
Source  : all_fixed.bin
Programs: 598

DLP is HP's proprietary instrument scripting language.
Statements are separated by ';'.  Control flow:
  IF(cond); <true>; ELSE; <false>; ENDIF;
  WHILE(cond); <body>; WEND;
  FUNCDEF name; <body>; ENDFUNC;
Variables: plain names = instrument registers (CF, SP, RL, …)
           __name = local DLP variable
